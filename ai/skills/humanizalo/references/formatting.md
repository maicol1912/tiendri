# Formatting tells

Complete reference for patterns P27-P31.

---

## Em dashes (P27)

Em dashes (—) are the single most reliable AI writing tell. AI uses them constantly. Most human writers rarely use them, and many don't know how to type them.

**Rule: No em dashes. Zero tolerance.**

Replace every em dash with either:
- A comma
- A period and new sentence
- Parentheses (sparingly)
- Restructure the sentence

**Examples:**
- "The team — which had been working for months — finally shipped" -> "The team, which had been working for months, finally shipped."
- "One thing was clear — they needed help" -> "One thing was clear. They needed help."

---

## Boldface and inline-header lists (P28)

AI mechanically emphasizes terms with **bold** and loves the "**Term:** explanation" list format.

**What to avoid:**
- Bold on every key term in a paragraph
- Lists formatted as "**Header:** Description of the thing" repeating down the page
- Bold used for emphasis more than once or twice per section

**Fix:**
- Use bold rarely. If everything is emphasized, nothing is.
- Restructure inline-header lists into prose paragraphs or simpler bullet points without the bold header format.
- Let the words do the emphasizing, not the formatting.

---

## Emojis in prose (P29)

AI adds decorative emojis to headings, bullet points, and section markers.

**Rule:** No emojis unless the original text had them or the context genuinely calls for them (e.g., a casual Slack message). Never in formal or semi-formal writing.

---

## Curly quotation marks (P30)

AI tends to produce "curly" or "smart" quotes. In many technical and web contexts, straight quotes are standard.

**Rule:** Match the convention of the target format. For code, technical docs, and most web content, use straight quotes. For published prose where the style guide calls for smart quotes, they're fine.

---

## Title Case in headings (P31)

AI defaults to Title Case Where Every Main Word Is Capitalized.

**Rule:** Use sentence case. Capitalize only the first word and proper nouns.

- AI: "The Future Of Artificial Intelligence In Healthcare"
- Human: "The future of artificial intelligence in healthcare"
